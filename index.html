What this word counter does

This free online word counter helps you count words, characters, sentences, and paragraphs in real time. It is useful for essays, articles, blog posts, scripts, assignments, and social media drafts.

Beyond basic counting, it also estimates reading time and speaking time, highlights long sentences, and shows frequently used words. That makes it more useful than a basic text counter and helps you improve clarity while writing.
